About CFEA

Career Foundation English Academy (CFEA) is a co-educational government recognized English medium school located in Napet Palli, Imphal East, Manipur (795010).

We provide quality education from Day Care up to Class X, with a proven track record of 100% success rate in board examinations.

Our mission is to nurture young minds with holistic development, combining academic excellence with moral values and life skills.
